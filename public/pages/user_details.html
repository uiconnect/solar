What to Expect During Home Energy Evaluation

The Evaluation typically takes about 30 minutes, where our Energy Specialist helps you understand how solar works and how it can help reduce your energy bill. Our Specialists will then visit your roof and prepare a rooftop layout to determine what System Size can be installed and the maximum energy potential of your home.
